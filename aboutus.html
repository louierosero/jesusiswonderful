Statement of the Faith

Through the balanced impartation of the word of God, Holy Spirit and Life. The Jesus Is Wonderful Ministry is committed to make disciples of all nations ( Matthew 28:19-20 ). And to tell all His wonderful acts for the end time harvest ( Psalm 105:2 ).

Our Mission

To make Disciples of All Nation.
( Matthew 28:19-20 )

Our Vision

To tell of All His Wonderful Acts.
( Psalm 105:2 )

Core Values

In our beliefs and actions as part of JIW's Ministry we hold in high regard.

Service

In union with Christ Jesus, then, I can be proud of my service. Romans 15:17

Integrity

May my goodness and honesty preserve me, because I trust in you. Psalm 25:21

Loyalty and Faithfulness

Never let go loyalty and faithfulness. Tie them around your neck; write them on you heart. Proverbs 3:3

Commitment

If your not willing to serve him, decide to today whom you will serve, the gods of your ancestors worshipped in Mesopotamia or the gods of the Amorites, in whose land you are now living. Joshua 24:15

Courage

Just be determine, be confident; and make sure that you obey the whole Law that my servant Moses gave you. Joshua 1:7

Remember that I have commanded you to be determined and confident! Do not be afraid or discourage, for I, the Lord your God, am with you wherever you go. Joshua 1:9

Competence

There is nothing in us that allows us to claim that we are capable of doing this work. Capacity we have comes from God; It is he who made us capable of serving the new covenant, which consists not of a written law but of the Spirit. The written law brings death, but the Spirit gives life. 2 Corinthians 3:5-6

Strategy

To help people to develop a personal relationship with God, to edify, encourage and equip them through the impartation of God's word and to train them for Goldy living and service.

Goal and Purposes

Our goal is to see people to become committed followers of our Lord Jesus Christ with Christ like character abounding in love for God and others.
Our purpose is to provide opportunities for yielded lives to be changed. Trained and equipped through the five fold ministry for the building-up of the end time church and for the meeting the needs of the world at large.

Office

The office worship and training center will be used to fulfill God's mission and vision for JIW's Ministry in transforming lives and communities throughout the world. The office will be used to train workers, members, student, lay people and preachers to become dynamic leaders.
